# FestVerse

## Purpose & Vision

### Our Mission
FestVerse simplifies how colleges organize, manage, and participate in events by providing one secure, modern platform for event registration, ticketing, attendance, payments, certificates, and analytics.

We replace manual Google Forms, spreadsheets, and paper attendance with a seamless digital experience.

---

## Problems We Solve
Most colleges currently use:
* **Google Forms** for registrations
* **Excel sheets** for participant lists
* **Manual payment verification** (checking screenshots/bank statements)
* **Printed attendance sheets**
* **Manual certificate creation** and distribution
* **WhatsApp groups** for announcement coordination

This process is slow, error-prone, and difficult to manage. **FestVerse automates the entire workflow.**

---

## What FestVerse Does

### For Students
* **Discover**: Find upcoming campus events easily.
* **Register**: Sign up for events in seconds.
* **Verify Identity**: Link student profiles securely using PRNs.
* **Secure Payments**: Pay securely for paid events with verified flows.
* **QR Ticketing**: Receive a secure, personalized QR ticket.
* **Attendance**: Seamless check-in and tracking.
* **Certificates**: Automatically download certificates of participation.
* **History**: View full participation and ticketing history.

### For Organizers
* **Event Creation**: Design, schedule, and publish events.
* **Registrations**: Manage signups and set participation limits.
* **Attendance**: Scan QR tickets on-site to track attendance in real time.
* **Announcements**: Send updates directly to participants.
* **Export Data**: Download participant lists and reports.
* **Certificate Issuance**: Automate certificate generation.

### For College Administration
* **Oversight**: Manage all college events from a central interface.
* **Analytics**: Monitor registration metrics, revenue, and real-time attendance.
* **Verification**: Verify student records and manage roles.
* **Access Control**: Manage organizer permissions.
* **Reports**: Generate audit logs and comprehensive reports.

---

## Core Features
* **Student Authentication**: Safe authentication using Google login.
* **PRN Verification**: Direct whitelist matching for student accounts.
* **Event Discovery & Registration**: Dynamic directory for free & paid events.
* **Secure QR Ticketing**: Tamper-proof check-in tokens.
* **QR Check-in & Attendance**: Real-time admin scanning interface.
* **Certificate Generation**: Automated, secure certificate issuance.
* **Analytics Dashboard**: Comprehensive data views for organizers and admins.
* **Reports & Exports**: CSV/Excel downloads for registrations.

---

## Security
* **Authentication**: Integrated Google Auth.
* **Access Control**: Row Level Security (RLS) in Supabase.
* **QR Security**: Cryptographically signed/secure QR tokens.
* **Verification**: Server-side verification of payments and registrations.
* **Auditing**: Audit logs and role restrictions.

---

## Future Vision
FestVerse is designed to scale from a single-college platform into a multi-college SaaS.
* **Multi-College SaaS**: Host multiple colleges under one application.
* **Branding**: Custom branding and subdomain/domain mappings for individual colleges.
* **Payment Gateways**: College-specific checkout integrations.
* **Inter-College Events**: Cross-campus discovery and registrations.
* **Certificate QR Verification**: Publicly shareable certificate verification system via unique QR codes.
* **Team/Group Registrations**: Structured flows for team registrations (e.g. for Hackathons, sports tournaments).
* **Calendar Integration**: Direct calendar sync (Google/Apple) for registered events.
* **LinkedIn & Social Sharing**: Integrated sharing templates for students to display achievements and participation on LinkedIn and Twitter.
* **Advanced Modules**: Sponsor management, alumni networks, volunteer portals, and native mobile apps.
* **AI Insights**: AI-powered event analytics and scheduling recommendations.
